## 4. Public-Benefit / ACeS Exception

### 4.1 Eligibility
The Revenue Cap in Section 3 does not apply to You if You satisfy **all** of the following objective criteria (each a "Qualifying Condition"):

**(a) Recognized Nonprofit or Public-Benefit Form.** You are incorporated or established under applicable law as a nonprofit corporation, charity, foundation, cooperative organized for public benefit, or a substantively equivalent entity form in Your jurisdiction that (i) is recognized by a government tax authority as tax-exempt or not-for-profit, or has submitted a complete application for such recognition that is pending, **and** (ii) prohibits distribution of net income or assets to private owners, members, or shareholders except upon dissolution as required by law.

**(b) No Private Benefit Distribution.** Your governing documents and operations do not permit, and You do not in practice make, distributions of profit or dividends to private individuals or for-profit entities, other than reasonable compensation for services rendered at fair-market rates.

**(c) Mission Alignment.** Your governing documents (e.g., articles of incorporation, constitution, or bylaws) state, as a primary purpose, a mission that is demonstrably consistent with enabling broad access to resources, technology, knowledge, or infrastructure in ways that reduce artificial scarcity and expand human flourishing, commonly described as an "Abundance Centered Society" (ACeS) or "Post-Scarcity Society" **and** Your actual operations are substantially and continuously aligned with that stated mission.

### 4.2 Valid Certification or Self-Certification
To invoke the exception in Section 4.1, You must:
1. Before or within 30 days of first invoking this exception, deliver to the Licensor at the contact address in Section 10 a written self-certification    that states: the legal name and jurisdiction of incorporation of Your    organization; the name of the relevant tax-exempt or non-profit status    granted or pending; and a plain-language description of how Your mission and operations satisfy each Qualifying Condition.

2. Update that self-certification promptly (and in any event within 30 days) if any material change occurs in Your status or operations that could affect Your eligibility.

Alternatively to self-certification you must obtain a valid certificate obtained from the Abundant Mars Foundation or Non-Reductionist Philosophy organisation 

### 4.3 Licensor Review
The Licensor may, acting in good faith, review Your self-certification and request additional supporting documentation. If the Licensor reasonably determines that You no longer satisfy all Qualifying Conditions, it will provide written notice specifying the basis for that determination. You will then have 60 days to cure any deficiency before the Revenue Cap applies.

The Section 4 exception applies only to the Revenue Cap. All other obligations under this License, including attribution (Section 5), trademark restrictions (Section 6), and the warranty/liability provisions (Section 7) continue to apply to Public-Benefit Organizations.
